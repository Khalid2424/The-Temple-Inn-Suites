About Us

The Temple Inn & Suites is a chain of hotels that specializes in accommodations for members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who are attending the temple. We provide stay and services to those who are attending, and those who are serving, such as full-time temple missionaries. Some of the services we provide include catering and venue for weddings, and temple clothing cleaning and shuttle for temple patrons.
